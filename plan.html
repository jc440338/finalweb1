Mission

Our mission is to develop a website which has two goals. First, we have to develop an effective website that can attract new members to join Townsville Community Music Centre and to encourage members' participation in the provided activities such as voluntering for administration, etc. Second, We expect an increase in the ticket sales for general public, persuade the participants to perform concerts that will let us be the organizer, and lastly, we aim to increase performers' participation to engage in promotion, photo shoots, media releases,etc.

Success Evaluation

The success of the website will be evaluated by observing the increase in sales and participants' engagement in the activities. Sucess Evaluation is listed below:

- Increase in new members registration

- Increase in member engagements

- Increase in ticket sales

- Survey evaluation

- More concerts

Target Audience

There is no specific target audience as Townsville Community Music Centre encourage participants of all ages and tastes to join, participants may have specialized styles for example, classical, jazz, ethnic music, etc. We aim to attract young group of people as the younger generation uses internet on a daily basis (mobile-access). As well as, children are our main target, hence, our website design should be easy to use, fast, convenient, and colorful. One of our objective is to attract specialise audience, hence, we use different kind of pictures to make audience feel excited.
